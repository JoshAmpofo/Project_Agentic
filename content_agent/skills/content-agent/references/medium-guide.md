# Medium Long-Form Guide

Medium mode is standalone: no planner, no daily-10. Produce ONE polished draft.

## Required structure
- **Title:** compelling, specific, curiosity- or value-driven.
- **Subtitle:** one line that earns the click and frames the payoff.
- **Body:**
  - Intro hook (2–4 sentences): a tension, story, or surprising claim.
  - 3–5 sections, each with a `##` subhead and substantive content.
  - Takeaway / close: what the reader should do or remember.
- **Read time:** estimate honestly (~200 words/min).
- **Tags:** exactly 5 suggested Medium tags.

## Tone
Authoritative but human. Concrete examples over abstractions. No filler intro
("In today's fast-paced world…"). Earn every paragraph.
